Some More About Me

When I'm not hacking away at things, I can almost always be found hanging out with my family -- my wife, LA, and our son, Robbie. That's them over there.

We enjoy the outdoors and spend our evenings taking walks or going on runs (though we do our fair share of binging on Netflix too).

A self-professed nerd, on the weekends I still try to find a little time to squeeze in a video game or two.

I am an avid sports fan, but having played soccer in high school, I live for the World Cup. It's one of the top items on my bucket list!

I have an ever-growing passion for reading, especially fantasy novels. I recently finished the Mistborn trilogy and am currently working on tackling The Stormlight Archives.
